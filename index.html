About The Project

Eldeco 7 Peaks Residences

Welcome to Eldeco 7 Peaks Residences — a landmark luxury development in Omicron 1A, Greater Noida. Spread across 7.5 acres, this ultra-low density project features just 7 standalone towers designed to offer resort-style living with infinite green views.

With architecture by globally acclaimed AEDAS (Hong Kong) and landscape design by Coopers Hill (Singapore), every home is a masterpiece. All 900 premium residences are 3-side open, facing greens or central tropical water features.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything You Need to Know

Where is Eldeco 7 Peaks Residences located? +
Eldeco 7 Peaks Residences is located in Omicron 1A, Greater Noida — one of the most strategically positioned sectors with access to the Noida-Greater Noida Expressway, Yamuna Expressway, Delta 1 Metro Station (Aqua Line), and the upcoming Jewar International Airport.
What is the starting price for 3 BHK at Eldeco 7 Peaks? +
3 BHK apartments at Eldeco 7 Peaks start from ₹2.35 Cr* onwards, with super areas ranging from 1825 to 2100 Sq.Ft across 5 different configurations (Type 1A, Type 2A, Grande 1, Grande 2, and Grande 3). 4 BHK configurations start from ₹3.65 Cr*.
Is Eldeco 7 Peaks RERA approved? +
Yes. Eldeco 7 Peaks Residences is RERA registered under UP RERA Registration Number: UPRERAPRJ106523/01/2026. The project was launched on 28th January 2026. You can verify this at the official UP RERA website: www.up-rera.in.
How many towers and apartments are there? +
Eldeco 7 Peaks features 7 standalone towers — Asgard, Blanc, Crestone, Denali, Everest, Kili West, and Kili East — spread across 7.5 acres. There are approximately 900 premium apartments in total with only 4 apartments per floor, ensuring ultra-low density and maximum privacy.
Who are the architects and landscape designers? +
The architecture is by AEDAS (Hong Kong) — one of the world's most celebrated architecture firms known for iconic luxury residential design. Landscape design is by Coopers Hill (Singapore) — experts in resort-style tropical landscaping, creating an oasis of greenery around every tower.
How many swimming pools are there? +
Eldeco 7 Peaks features 4 distinct swimming pools: a Lap Pool, a Tropical Leisure Pool, a Semi-Outdoor Heated Pool (for year-round use), and a Kids' Pool with a sand beach — a truly resort-like experience within your residential complex.
